About the company

moodya! is a social entertainment startup that was founded to bring style, grace and fun to your facebook profile photos. With just the right mix of creativity, IT and business knowledge in our team we might as well be one of the leading fun and style companies of the future.

Bringing together profound design competence, state of the art technology and the largest social network in the world with your individual daily mood, we believe we can change the face of facebook! Not too much too soon you will be looking at your facebook news feed and discover that your friends are no longer just posting messages but directly expressing their moods with their profile pictures.

moodya! is the 1st company to give you the possibility to express yourself in a totally different way, every day, with just a few clicks!
